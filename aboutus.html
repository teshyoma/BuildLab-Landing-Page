About Us

BuildLab Africa is a Talent accelerator that empowers and upskills entry-level African Tech and creative talents by providing them with Project bootcamps and Internship placements, to help them gain professional experience, hone their skills, build their portfolios, grow their network and assist their transitioning into the industry. We are passionate and driven to address the talent gap and unemployment challenges across Africa. Our program module provides industry-specific exposure, training and experiential learning opportunities, strong understanding of what it's like to work in a Tech startup/Company and obtain the hands-on experience necessary for their chosen professional career.
